This sconce brings an overlooked but ubiquitous part of the urban landscape into the home. These outdoor pipe “shields” can be found outside most commercial buildings, but blend into their surroundings as they are placed low to the ground. In mounting this found object on the wall and using it as a lamp, it becomes a prominent and hyper-visible part of the room.

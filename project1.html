Sketches & Initial Concepts

When I first began conceiving of this project, my reflections centered around touch deprivation. I explored wearable methods of distancing which could alleviate touch deprivation by providing compression for the user, while also expanding outward to create a barrier and measuring stick. This idea required two users to wear the suit, thereby creating the necessary distance while also simulating a moment of physical intimacy between them. I also explored inflation, specifically self-inflating technologies, for the purpose of immediacy.

Seating

After exploring wearables, I settled on expandable seating as a way of creating distance while also maintaining a link between those who would otherwise be sitting together.

Fabrication Process

I was inspired by the irregularity of concrete mould tubes to create a collapsible chair. Using only window insulation tap and friction, I create a mechanism to collapse and expand the bench.
